Anissa Pooley | WDD 130

Hello, I am Anissa Pooley. I live in Carlin, Nevada. I am a mom of five wonderful kids. I enjoy playing piano and singing. I also love to paint and draw. I am currently teaching my youngest two kids to play the piano. They are loving it. We love to go out as a family and explore the mountains of Nevada. We often explore ghost towns and find items left from the old mines. Of course, we have to be very careful and watch out for abandoned mine shafts. Most have been fenced off, but every now and then we find one that has been forgotten.
