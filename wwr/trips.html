Blue River Ride

As part of the Walker River near Yerington, Nevada, Blue River Ride is the most requested trip by our clients. It is a grade III white water ride. Not too easy, not too hard, it takes an average of 6 hours to complete.

Meet us in Reno, Nevada for this grade IV ride. White Water Gorge offers a challenge for more seasoned rafters. The trip averages 2 days. We have all the gear you need. Please contact us with any questions about rentals.
