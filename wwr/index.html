Our Purpose

Time goes by. Days pile up and mesh together. Our goal at Great OutdOARS is to help you take a step away from the grind, to help you get away to spend time with the people you value most. By getting outside and into the hills and mountains of Northern Nevada, you will be able to relax and reset. You will feel rejuvinated and your time will have been well spent!

What We Offer

After you schedule one of our adventures, we pretty much take it from there. As some of the rides require some traveling, we provide the transportation. Our river guides stick with you for the entire experience. We realize that there are many different skill levels. We have river rides that range from basic, quiet floats to Grade IV white water rapids. We understand that along with a variety of skill levels comes a need for a variety of gear. We have everything you need!
